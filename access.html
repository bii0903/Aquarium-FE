Access

For those coming by free shuttle bus

JR Keihin Tohoku Line "Oimachi station" Central ticket gate (Atre mouth), Tokyu Oimachi line "Oiicho station", Rinkai line "Oi-machi station" A1 Exit from East exit Bus stop 6th stop from Bus stop Please note that there is also a bus rotary at the West Exit ※.

For those coming by car

★ Parking fee Regular car: 100 yen (per 20 minutes) * Since the number of parking spaces is limited, you may have to wait if the parking lot is full. * If you would like to park the bus, please check "For groups". * Saturdays, Sundays, and holidays are very crowded. We recommend that you use public transportation such as trains as it will fill up early..
